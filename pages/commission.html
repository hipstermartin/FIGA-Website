Commission

It was decided to formulate the following commissions to discuss and address the pertinent issue in their representative fields:

  • Geosciences Education
  • Climate and Global Changes
  • Mineral and Energy Resources
  • Mountain Dynamics
  • Marine Geosciences
  • Water Resources
  • Geohazards

The scope of these Commissions is to initiate collective discussions bring in various associations and the interested scientific community to assess the present status, envisage the future requirements and design an action plan to address them using a scientific approach. These commissions would be organizing a number of events including seminars, symposia, and workshops with the involvement and support of member geosciences organizations, and would disseminate the outcome for the attention of the scientific community as well as the concerned Government agencies. The commissions also would be looking at developing major scientific initiatives to address the contemporary needs of geosciences in the country as a whole.

The FIGA apart from actively coordinating and working with the commissions would organize a tri-annual congress wherein the member associations are also expected to have their annual meetings being held concurrently. However, FIGA will not be interfering with the set agenda of the member associations but would be very much interested to put challenges faced by the geoscientific community together and coming up with comprehensive strategies backed by a scientific approach to address them. The commissions have designed their own objectives and action plans with the framework of FIGA and the same are being discussed and implemented from time to time.
